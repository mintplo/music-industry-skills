# Circle Chart

## Capabilities

Find Korean Circle Chart rankings and distinguish the site's Album and Retail
Album chart contexts, plus certification pages when directly relevant.

## Use when

Support a claim about a specifically named Circle chart, market, chart period,
rank, or value that is explicitly displayed on an official Circle page.

## Do not use for

Treating rank as sales, treating album shipment/distribution measures as retail
sales, or converting one Circle chart type into another without documented
methodology.

## Access

Use public official pages at [Circle Chart](https://circlechart.kr/). If a
needed page route is undocumented, use it only at low frequency through normal
browser access; do not probe routes, evade controls, or automate against site
restrictions.

## Inputs and outputs

Start with artist, title, chart type, and weekly, monthly, or yearly period.
Retain the chart name, market, period, rank or displayed value, edition/title,
page URL, observation date, and any measurement definition shown by Circle.

## Evidence

Authority tier: operator-published Korean chart and certification evidence for
the exact page and period cited. Cite the direct official page; label the chart
type and preserve shipment/distribution versus retail provenance.

## Limits and terms

Circle exposes separate Album and Retail Album chart branches. The site also
states that unauthorized AI/ML training or text and data mining is prohibited;
respect that restriction, robots directives, terms, and rate limits.

## Fallbacks

Use an official certification or label/press source for the stated claim, then
[Web search](web-search.md) to locate it. If the chart value or definition is
not directly available, report it as unavailable.

## Verification

Check chart type, period, artist and edition before reporting a result. Never
present ranks, shipment/distribution measures, and retail sales as comparable
without matching definitions.
