# Research Music Forward Cases

1. 코르티스의 새 앨범이 어떤 앨범인지, 콘셉트와 공개 후 초기 반응을 조사해줘.
2. aespa의 LEMONADE 컴백에서 첫 티저부터 발매 후 일주일까지 프로모션 흐름을 조사해줘.
3. aespa와 IVE의 2025~2026년 일본 시장 반응을 같은 기준으로 비교해줘. 비교가 안 되는 수치는 분리해줘.
4. 최근 K-pop 걸그룹의 선공개 싱글 활용 방식에 공통 패턴이 있는지 조사해줘.
5. aespa의 Whiplash가 처음 발매된 날짜와 수록 앨범만 알려줘.

Success signals:

- Cases 1–4 name or visibly use more than one relevant provider family.
  Web discovery and the direct pages it finds count as one family; crawling is
  distinct only when that access method is actually used.
- Case 5 stays short and does not generate a full artist report.
- Every case closes unavailable branches explicitly rather than inventing data.
- No case loads unrelated provider cards or forces all recipes.
- Every performance number preserves its source URL, market, period,
  measurement definition, observation date, and official or unofficial status.
  These are semantic requirements, not mandatory visible labels or a fixed
  presentation schema.
- Quantitative comparisons use compatible markets, metrics, units, measurement
  types, periods, and windows. Incompatible or unmatched evidence stays
  separate and cannot determine a winner.
- Raw sales volume is not called `conversion` without a denominator that makes
  conversion measurable.
- Qualitative reaction and demand claims are attributed, keep their sampling
  and interpretation limits visible, and use claim-specific support.
- Performance branches use the fewest decision-relevant metrics needed to
  answer the question rather than expanding into a metric dump.
- Material claims use direct claim-specific pages; generic indexes are
  discovery aids, not sole evidence.
- Case 2 gives claim-specific support for material teaser or MV claims when
  available and qualifies access-dependent or unavailable campaign evidence.
- Case 4 defines a bounded sample and window, uses supporting examples plus
  meaningful exceptions or counterexamples, states sampling limits, and avoids
  universal or causal overclaims.
- Relative windows such as initial or early are defined before collection;
  later momentum is identified as outside that window rather than used as
  evidence for the requested early-response branch.
